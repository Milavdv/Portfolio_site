Philips Root

Oral Health Improving Smart Toy

Meet Root, a lovable friend that will stand beside and empower you to take care of your oral health in a playful, clumsy way.

For teens, keeping up with oral health can be hard. The biggest issue is the dislike for the activity; getting distracted by their phone, rushing through it or skipping it altogether.

Root is a supportive buddy-like smart toy. It heightens your psychological investment by making progress visible and provides a fun way to maintain oral health by letting you pull the strings.

Teenagers don’t seem to brush well and can be hard to motivate. In this project for Philips Research, the aim is to explore the possibility of using smart toys to stimulate emotional engagement and make oral hygiene practices more playful, conscious, and effective. Desk and field research are combined in a thesis; advising Philips in designing for their unexplored target group, and used for the smart toy concept Root.

Root provides positive distraction during brushing. Instead of looking at your phone, you can watch the creature crawl, dance, and slowly stand up until each area of the mouth is thoroughly cleaned. In addition to this, Root uses a streak to heighten emotional investment. Root works together with Philips’ series of smart toothbrushes.
